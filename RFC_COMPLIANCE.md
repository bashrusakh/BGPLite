## RFC 4893 — 4-octet AS Number Space
**Relevance:** required · **Reference:** https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc4893

Core 4-byte AS support is correctly implemented: AS4 capability (65) is always advertised and parsed, OPEN MyAS uses real ASN <=65535 or AS_TRANS 23456 when >65535, and AS_PATH is encoded/parsed in both 2- and 4-byte forms; AS4_PATH/AS4_AGGREGATOR reconstruction is intentionally absent (route-server does not re-advertise) but receive-side AS_PATH/segment validation and the NLRI prefix-length>32 guard are missing.

## RFC 2545 — BGP-4 MP for IPv6
**Relevance:** optional · **Reference:** https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc2545

BGPLite is uniformly IPv4-only and implements essentially NO RFC 2545 functionality: no AFI=2 capability, no IPv6 NLRI codec, no MP_REACH/MP_UNREACH (attr 14/15), no IPv6 global/link-local next-hop, and the IpPrefix/Route/RouteTable types cannot represent IPv6 — all 15 MUST/SHOULD rows fail (none=11, partial=4).
